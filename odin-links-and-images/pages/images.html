Image Formats

This page features different types of image formats

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos, since they're good at depicting color, but cannot depict transparent pixels

GIFs

GIFs are great for animation, however they can't depict color as effectively as JPGs

PNGs

PNGs are great for diagrams and icons, since they deal with color palettes and opacity just fine, but lack in file size optimization

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. They can display images without any loss of quality on scaling. Use them wherever you can.
